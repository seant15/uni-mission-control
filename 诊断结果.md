5. 添加以下变量:

| Key | Value | Environments |
|-----|-------|--------------|
| `VITE_USE_MOCK_DATA` | `false` | Production, Preview, Development |
| `VITE_OPENCLAW_GATEWAY_URL` | `http://open.unippc24.com:9090` | Production, Preview, Development |
